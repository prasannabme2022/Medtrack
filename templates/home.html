How It Works

From Upload to Insight in Seconds

Our end-to-end AI pipeline processes your data through AWS services to deliver structured, actionable health predictions.

1
Create Account
Sign up as a patient or doctor. Your profile is securely stored in Amazon DynamoDB.
2
Upload Data
Upload medical images, describe symptoms, input vitals, or attach lab report PDFs.
3
AI Processes
S3 stores files → Rekognition & Textract extract features → Comprehend Medical identifies entities.
4
Bedrock Predicts
Claude on Amazon Bedrock synthesizes all signals into a structured clinical prediction report.
5
Doctor Alerted
For High/Critical cases, Amazon SNS instantly notifies the doctor via email and SMS.
